About Us

The Beauty Room is an award winning salon based in the small town of Cardigan, West Wales. Our team are some of the best in the industry, bringing years of experience and knowledge to The Beauty Room.

We offer a range of treatments from nails to waxing which are all reasonably priced, our aim is to be affordable whilst maintaining great service and products. We can accommodate group bookings either in salon or mobile, please enquire for further information.

To book an appointment please complete the form on our Contact Us page, we will respond to your request as soon as possible. Alternatively, you can call us on 01239456654 or e-mail thebeautyroom@email.com to book your appointment today.
